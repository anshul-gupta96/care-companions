FAQ

How can I hire a housemaid/babysitter/cook from care companions ?

1:Choose your desired service, fill in your requirements and make a booking on our platform.
2: Confirm your requirements with the relationship manager assigned to you.
3: Sit tight while our relationship manager finds the right fit for your home.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

While all our helpers undergo an intensive training regime before being certified as a Broomee, we still believe that there’s always room for improvement. In order to ensure that you obtain the optimum fit for your home, we provide an ‘complimentary replacement policy’ as part of your subscription. Just reach out to your relationship manager or mail us carecompanion@gmail.com.

Will I always have the same service providers for each scheduled service?

We make every effort to provide you with the same service providers each and every time we clean your home, but there are times that a change is required due to sickness, injury, etc.
